Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity starts from 500 pieces, but we can discuss smaller quantities for initial orders or sample production.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 15-30 days depending on order complexity and quantity. Rush orders can be accommodated with additional fees.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for approval before mass production. Sample costs and timeline will be discussed based on your requirements.

What customization options are available?

We offer extensive customization including materials, colors, sizes, logos, labels, and special features to match your brand requirements.

What are your payment terms?

We typically require 30% deposit with order confirmation and 70% balance before shipment. Other payment terms can be negotiated for established clients.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes, we ship to clients worldwide. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure safe and timely delivery to your location.
