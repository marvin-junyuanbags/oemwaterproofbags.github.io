Quality Control Process

1

Material Inspection

All incoming materials undergo rigorous inspection including fabric strength testing, waterproof coating verification, and hardware durability checks.

  • Fabric tensile strength testing
  • Waterproof rating verification (IPX ratings)
  • Hardware corrosion resistance testing
  • Color fastness evaluation
2

Production Monitoring

Continuous quality monitoring throughout the production process with multiple checkpoints to ensure consistency.

  • Cutting precision verification
  • Stitching quality inspection
  • Seam sealing effectiveness testing
  • Assembly accuracy checks
3

Waterproof Testing

Comprehensive waterproof testing using industry-standard methods to ensure complete water resistance.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Spray testing for surface water resistance
  • Submersion testing for full waterproofing
  • Seam integrity evaluation
4

Durability Testing

Extensive durability testing to ensure products can withstand real-world usage conditions.

  • Zipper cycle testing (10,000+ cycles)
  • Strap strength and endurance testing
  • Abrasion resistance testing
  • Temperature resistance evaluation
5

Final Inspection

Comprehensive final inspection covering all aspects of product quality before packaging and shipment.

  • Visual appearance inspection
  • Functional testing of all features
  • Packaging quality verification
  • Documentation and labeling accuracy

Testing Standards & Methods

Waterproof Testing

IPX Rating Tests

Testing according to IEC 60529 standards for various levels of water protection (IPX4, IPX6, IPX7, IPX8).

Hydrostatic Pressure

Testing water resistance under pressure using AATCC Test Method 127 with pressures up to 10,000mm water column.

Spray Testing

Surface water resistance testing using ISO 4920 standard spray test method.

Durability Testing

Tensile Strength

Fabric and strap strength testing according to ASTM D5034 standard test method.

Abrasion Resistance

Martindale abrasion testing according to ISO 12947 standard for fabric durability.

Zipper Durability

Zipper cycle testing according to ASTM D2061 standard with minimum 10,000 cycles.

Safety & Compliance

Chemical Safety

Testing for restricted substances according to REACH regulation and CPSIA requirements.

Color Fastness

Color stability testing according to ISO 105 standards for light, washing, and rubbing fastness.

Flammability

Fire resistance testing according to relevant safety standards for different markets.
