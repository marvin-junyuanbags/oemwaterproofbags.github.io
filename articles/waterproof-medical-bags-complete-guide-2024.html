Frequently Asked Questions

Are waterproof medical bags suitable for sterile equipment storage?

+

Yes, when properly designed with antimicrobial materials and sealed compartments. However, sterile equipment should remain in original sterile packaging until use, with the bag providing additional protection.

How do I clean and disinfect waterproof medical bags?

+

Follow manufacturer guidelines using approved medical disinfectants. Most bags can be wiped down with hospital-grade disinfectants, while some allow for more thorough cleaning methods.

What size medical bag do I need for different healthcare roles?

+

Nurses: 15-25L for supplies and basic equipment. EMTs: 35-45L for comprehensive emergency supplies. Specialists: 20-40L depending on equipment requirements. Consider your daily supply needs and transport method.

Do waterproof medical bags meet regulatory requirements?

+

Quality medical bags from reputable manufacturers meet relevant healthcare regulations including FDA, ISO, and international standards. Always verify compliance for your specific healthcare environment and requirements.
