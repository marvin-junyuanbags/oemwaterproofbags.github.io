Frequently Asked Questions

What size pet carrier do I need for my pet?
Measure your pet's length, width, and height. The carrier should allow your pet to stand, turn around, and lie down comfortably. Add 2-4 inches to each measurement for proper sizing. Most carriers specify weight and dimension limits for different pet sizes.
Are waterproof pet carriers safe for air travel?
Yes, when they meet airline requirements. Look for carriers with TSA approval, proper ventilation, secure locking mechanisms, and dimensions that fit under airline seats. Always check with your specific airline for their pet carrier requirements.
How do I clean and maintain a waterproof pet carrier?
Remove all bedding and accessories. Clean with pet-safe disinfectants and warm water. Allow complete air drying before storage. Check zippers, seams, and ventilation regularly. Replace worn components to maintain waterproof integrity.

Can I use a waterproof pet carrier for multiple pets?
Only if the carrier is specifically designed for multiple pets and each pet has adequate space. Most carriers are designed for single pets to ensure proper ventilation, comfort, and safety. Consider separate carriers for better pet welfare.
What should I pack in my pet's travel carrier?
Include familiar bedding, a favorite toy, water and food bowls, leash, waste bags, health certificates, and any required medications. Avoid overpacking to ensure your pet has adequate space and ventilation.
How do I help my pet get comfortable with their carrier?
Introduce the carrier gradually at home. Place treats, toys, and meals inside to create positive associations. Start with short periods and gradually increase time. Never force your pet into the carrier, as this can create negative associations.
Are there weight limits for waterproof pet carriers?
Yes, each carrier has specific weight limits for pet safety and structural integrity. Exceeding weight limits can compromise safety features and waterproof seals. Always check manufacturer specifications and choose carriers appropriate for your pet's current and projected weight.
